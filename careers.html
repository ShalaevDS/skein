our culture

At Skein we’ve created an environment where the talented and innovative thrive and where the freedom to create is supported. Here you have an opportunity to be a part of building something truly unique. You will be constantly learning, experimenting, and growing as an individual and as a part of a close knit team. A spirit of collaboration and teamwork underlies everything we do. Together, we’re not just a work team, but we’re a team of friends. At Skein you are understood and supported as you become the best you can be.

Language classes

We’re an international team with international clients. As such, part of being great at our work is being good conversationalists with our clients. So, you’ll have access to free custom-designed language classes if needed.

comfortable office

To create amazing work, you need comfortable working conditions. We do our best to make sure you have everything you need.

Events and Conferences

Constant learning and development is embedded into our approach to work. We sponsor meetups, hackathons, conferences and seminars and actively encourage our team members to participate and to organize such events themselves.

Flexible Working

We all work at our own pace. You may be just getting into stride when most people are calling it a night. Or maybe something’s changed in your life and turned your schedule upside down. Whatever the circumstances, we support flexible hours and remote working, so you can manage your life and your job.

Friendly team

You are an individual, and you want a little personal space and distance to think and work. But you also like knowing that help is just a chair away. Well, welcome home. We’ve got you covered on both those needs. Our team is a small family, and we put a lot of importance on maintaining a positive, supportive and fun environment.
