our culture

At Skein we have created an environment where the talented and innovative thrive. We support the freedom to create. Here you have an opportunity to be a part of building something truly unique. You will be constantly learning, experimenting, and growing as an individual and as a part of a close knit team. Our spirit of collaboration and teamwork underlines everything we do. Together, we are not just a work team, but we are a team of friends. At Skein you are understood and supported as you become the best you can be.

benefits of working at skein

Paid vacation and unlimited unpaid leave

Are you a workaholic? Or do you need time to unwind to get your creative juices flowing? Skein accommodates all working styles. You’ll receive two weeks paid vacation a year. If you need more time away from the office, just plan in advance and make sure the projects you are working on will not be affected.

Language classes

We are an international team with international clients. As such, part of being great at our work is being good conversationalists with our clients. Our team enjoys free custom-designed language classes.

comfortable office

To create amazing work, you need comfortable working conditions. We do our best to make sure you have everything you need.

Events and Conferences

Constant learning and development is embedded into our approach to work. We sponsor meetups, hackathons, conferences and seminars and actively encourage our team members to participate and to organize such events themselves.

Flexible Working

We all work at our own pace. You may be just getting into stride when most people are calling it a night. Or maybe something’s changed in your life and turned your schedule upside down. Whatever the circumstances, we support flexible hours and remote working, so you can manage your life and feel comfortable working.

Friendly team

You are an individual, and you need a little personal space and distance to think and work. But you also like knowing that, anytime you need it, help is just a chair away. Well, welcome home. We’ve got you covered on both those needs.Our team is a small family, and we put a lot of importance on maintaining a positive, supportive and fun environment.
